Automatic plant watering system :

The Automatic Plant Watering System is an innovative project utilizing Arduino Uno to automate the irrigation process. This system ensures that plants receive the right amount of water by monitoring soil moisture levels with a sensor. When the soil moisture drops below a predefined threshold, the Arduino triggers a water pump to hydrate the plants, ensuring optimal growth. This project not only reduces manual effort but also conserves water by delivering precise amounts as needed. Ideal for both small gardens and large agricultural setups, it exemplifies smart technology integration in horticulture, promoting sustainable and efficient plant care.

Drone Detecting System :

In my Second semester, I completed a project titled "Drone Detecting System." This system utilizes Python's OpenCV module to efficiently identify and track drones in real-time. By leveraging computer vision techniques, the system processes video feeds to detect the presence of drones, marking their positions and tracking their movements. The primary objective was to develop a robust and accurate detection system capable of operating in various environments. Through this project, I gained hands-on experience with Python programming and the practical application of OpenCV for real-world problems, enhancing my skills in both areas.
